Who we are

It's not just beauty.
It's the experience.

The Beauty Bar has been Pittsburg's go-to downtown salon for women who want more than a haircut. We're a full-service team that genuinely cares about how you feel when you leave our chair — not just how you look.

I

Your vision, executed.

We listen before we cut. Every consultation starts with your lifestyle, your Pinterest board, and your honest answer to "how much time do you actually spend on your hair?"

II

Downtown, always.

We're proud to be part of Broadway. A local business serving local people — from PSU students to the families who've called Pittsburg home for generations.

III

All services, one place.

Hair, nails, lashes, brows — you don't need four different appointments. Come in for everything, or just the one thing. We've got you either way.
